User Guide

Welcome to the Eymistaken's HUD documentation for players. This guide will walk you through setting up, configuring, and utilizing all the features of the mod for version 1.21.11.

Accessing the HUD Editor

Hot Tip: Pressing Right Shift (RShift) seamlessly opens the HUD Editor from anywhere in-game.

From the HUD Editor, you can visualize all your active modules. You can click and drag elements to precisely place them anywhere on your screen using our smart snapping and stacking algorithm. Modules can be snapped to Corners, Edges, or the Center.

The Keystrokes Designer

The crown jewel of Eymistaken's HUD is the Keystrokes Designer. This tool lets you completely reimagine how your keystrokes look and feel.

How to use the Designer

  1. Open the configuration menu and navigate to the Keystrokes Module settings.
  2. Click on the Keystrokes Designer button.
  3. Drag and Drop: Left-click and drag any key panel to move it around. They will intelligently snap to alignment grids for ultimate precision.
  4. Context Menu: Right-click anywhere in the designer to open a powerful context menu. Here you can add brand new keys manually to the display.
  5. Edit Labels: Select the "Edit Label" option to completely rename a key. Once active, you can use your keyboard's arrow keys to nudge the text pixel-by-pixel for perfect centering.
  6. Undo/Redo History: Made a mistake? Press CTRL + Z to undo or CTRL + Y to redo any movement or change. The system saves snapshots automatically.
